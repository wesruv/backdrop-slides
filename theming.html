Backdrop CMS Theming

api.backdropcms.org/developing-themes

Major differences from
 Drupal 7's theme layer:

  • 's html.tpl.php Backdrop's page.tpl.php
  • 's page.tpl.php Backdrop's Layout system
  • Layout markup are removed from themes
  • No more region template layer
